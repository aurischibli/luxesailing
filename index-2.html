Common Questions

Who enjoys these tours most?
Travelers (couples, families, friends) who want a comfortable, genuine Greek holiday without the usual crowds or planning stress. Good for people who like unique trips and prefer not to manage logistics.
What makes Luxe Sailing different?
It's our local knowledge, focus on real Greek food and places, smooth door-to-door service, comfy small-group style, and great local crew. We're not like DIY travel or big bus tours.
Are flights included?
We arrange all needed transfers *within* Greece for a smooth trip. Most guests book their own flights to Greece, but we can help book internal Greek flights or work with travel partners for international flights if you need it.
Do I need travel insurance?
Yes. For your protection, all guests must have travel insurance covering cancellations, medical needs, and belongings.
How much planning do I do?
Almost none! Just tell us your dates, group size, and what you like. We handle the detailed planning, booking, and logistics. We'll give you a full plan to approve.
