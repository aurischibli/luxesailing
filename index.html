Your Questions Answered

Who are these tours best for?
Discerning travellers (couples, families, friends) seeking authentic, comfortable, and hassle-free Greek experiences away from the typical tourist crowds. Ideal for those who value unique experiences and prefer not to handle complex logistics themselves.
What makes Luxe Sailing different?
Our deep local expertise, focus on authenticity (real food, hidden gems), seamless door-to-door service, commitment to luxury within a small-group setting, and experienced local crew set us apart from DIY travel or large-scale tour operators.
Are flights included?
We manage all necessary transfers *within* Greece as part of your tour package for a seamless experience. While most guests arrange their own international flights, we can certainly assist with booking internal Greek flights or coordinate with our trusted travel partners for international airfare if needed – just let us know!
Is travel insurance mandatory?
Yes, for your peace of mind and protection against unforeseen circumstances, all guests are required to have comprehensive travel insurance covering trip cancellation, medical emergencies, and personal belongings.
How much planning do *I* need to do?
Very little! Your main task is to tell us your desired travel dates, group size, and general preferences during our consultation. From there, we handle the intricate itinerary planning, bookings, and logistics, presenting you with a complete plan for approval.
